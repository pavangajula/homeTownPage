Visit Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital of Telangana and an indian city that blends history with modern technology.

Top three activities to do at Hyderabad

The historic Charminar monument in Hyderabad

Charminar was built by Sultan Mohammed Quli Qutb Shah in 1591. It was built in honour of his wife, Bhagmati.

The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa Hyderabad

The Salar Jung Museum is famous for its vast collection of art and artifacts from around the world.

The Golconda Fort is a majestic testament to history and architectural grandeur.

Golconda fort was originally known as Mankal, and built on a hilltop in the year 1143.
